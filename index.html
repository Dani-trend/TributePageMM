Marilyn Monroe

"Icon Muse Star"

ICON

information:

June 1, 1926

Norma Jean Mortenson is born in Los Angeles, California.

1935

Mortenson's mother has a schizophrenic breakdown and is institutionalized.

August 1945

Mortenson is signed to Blue Book Model Agency after being recommended by David Conover.

August 1946

Mortenson gets a six-month contract with 20th Century Fox and executive Ben Lyon chooses her screen name of "Marilyn Monroe."

1950

Her casting in The Asphalt Jungle earns her her first critical praise and launches her career.

1952

The "Cheesecake Queen" becomes this year's "it girl."

January 14, 1954

Monroe is married to New York Yankee center-fielder Joe DiMaggio.

September 1954

Monroe's famous white dress is captured in The Seven Year Itch.

July 1958

She stars in Some Like It Hot, a film critically acclaimed to this day.

August 5, 1962

Monroe is found dead after suicide by depressant overdose.
